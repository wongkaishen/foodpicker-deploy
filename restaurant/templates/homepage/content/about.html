Our Story

FoodPicker was born from a simple idea: As students at MMU, we’ve all faced the same problem—spending too much time deciding where to eat after class. Cyberjaya has plenty of food spots, yet people often say, “There’s nothing good to eat here.” With so many restaurants tucked away or overshadowed, finding the perfect place that fits our cravings and budget can feel like a real challenge. We’d go back and forth, scrolling through food delivery apps or asking friends for suggestions, only to end up at the same places over and over again. That endless debate? We’ve all been there. But for me, this journey started even earlier. Back when I was still in my foundation class, I built a simple pizza ordering system using Python during our MiniIT class. It wasn’t groundbreaking, but it opened my eyes to how technology could solve everyday problems—and it planted the seed that would grow into something more. That’s how FoodPicker came to life—a platform designed to cut through the noise and actually help students discover good food around Cyberjaya.

Our goal is to make food discovery effortless. Whether you’re after a quick bite, a cozy place to chill, or a hidden gem with the best student promos, FoodPicker has your back. By combining real recommendations, smart search filters, and data-driven suggestions, FoodPicker helps you skip the guesswork and go straight to good eats. No more “I don’t know where to eat.” No more settling for the same old spots. Let’s make food discovery easy. Let’s make every meal count.

Welcome to FoodPicker.
